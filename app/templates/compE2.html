Oriental Sounds?

Basic Information

Mini-Challenge #7: Oriental Sounds? (Lunar New Year Special Event)
Timeline: 2023.01.16 ~ 2023.02.12

Description: When Lunar New Year comes too early and the X-mas Spirit is still around, we can hear a mixture of X-mas music and LNY music being played all around us. Enjoy this set, specially crafted to celebrate a mixture of X-mas sound and Oriental sound.

Scoring System: EX-Score
EX-Score = (perfect + 0.8*great + 0.5*good + 0.1*bad) / (perfect + great + good + bad + miss)

Featured Songs

  1. Ai, Yurete... D8 Oriental
  2. Chinese Restaurant S10 Oriental
  3. BSPower Explosion S11 Oriental
  4. Tantanmen S14 Oriental
  5. X-tree D15 X-mas
  6. Rolling Christmas D16 X-mas

Low Boss: Christmas Memories S14 X-mas

High Boss: Utsushiyo no Kaze D18 Oriental

Threshold: 11

Mechanics

  1. Choose 3 songs out of the 6 songs in the list. Recommended to choose charts that suit your skill level!
  2. Add up the three song numbers, e.g. if you choose Ai Yurete D8, X-tree D15 and Rolling Christmas D16, your total will be 1 + 5 + 6 = 12
  3. Play the high boss if your total is equal to or more than the threshold. Play the low boss otherwise!

Objectives

Attain the highest value of (sum of player's 4 songs)

Other Information

Additional information for the mini-challenge are as follows:

  • Players are allowed to attempt each chart as many times as possible within the time period, and only the set of 4 songs with the highest total score will be used in the final calculation.
  • Currently, there is no requirement to upload the photo of your scores. However, please fill in the form provided here honestly.
Enjoy the competition, and may the odds be ever in your favor.
